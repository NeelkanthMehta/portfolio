MSc Capstone · SSRN Published
Piotroski F-Score + Market Timing Strategy
WorldQuant University · Python · Equity Long/Short
A systematic equity strategy combining Piotroski F-Score fundamental screening with quantitative market-timing signals. The framework filters for financially improving companies, then gates entries using macro regime indicators to reduce drawdown during adverse market conditions.
Key Result

Achieved strong out-of-sample risk-adjusted returns against benchmark across multiple backtest windows, with the timing overlay meaningfully reducing maximum drawdown.

Algorithmic Trading · Live-Ready
Multi-Regime Momentum Strategy
QuantConnect LEAN · SPY & NIFTY 50 · Hourly Resolution
A systematic momentum strategy with dual-signal volatility regime classification (VIX + realised vol), ATR-based trailing stops, triple-gated re-entry conditions, and inverse-volatility position sizing. NIFTY 50 exposure implemented via NIFTYBEES on NSE through Zerodha/TrueData integration.
Key Design Choices

Regime-aware sizing that scales exposure inversely to volatility, reducing drawdowns during high-VIX environments while preserving upside participation during trending regimes.

Banking Research · Regulatory Capital
Indian Banking Sector — Risk & Capital Adequacy
SBI · HDFC Bank · Bank of Baroda · Basel III/IV Framework
An eight-dimension comparative analysis of India's major banks covering CET1 adequacy, D-SIB surcharge impact, Pillar 2 MVE sensitivity, IRRBB framework compliance, and trapped capital dynamics. Produced as a structured analytical report with master metrics, rankings, and policy implications.
Key Finding

Identified SBI's ~49bps CET1 deficit against its post-surcharge effective minimum and HDFC's outsized Pillar 2 MVE sensitivity relative to its Pillar 1 market risk capital — both with direct capital planning implications.
